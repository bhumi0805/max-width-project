    Welcome to Ananya Vidyalaya
    - Zundal

    Ananya Vidyalaya is a co-educational English Medium school that follows the CBSE syllabus.

    The motive of the school is to avail the children with the best standards of learning, excellence in academics and co-curricular activities. Personal attention is given to individual’s unique potential and each student is treated to be unique. The method of teaching is always child - oriented and the learning process is conducted in warm and friendly atmosphere. We inculcate moral values through various practices and would our children in making the best and responsible citizens of India. We at Ananya Vidyalaya believe in the equality of all the children, hence each child is nurtured and guided in a perfect way that, the glory of an individual blossoms into their true potential.

    Why Ananya Vidyalaya?

    We are located on a scenic campus with lots of open space and fresh air within the serene and beautiful, fast developing Zundal area. We are co-educational school and have successfully presented our students for various competitions and intellectual activities.

    commerce

    +

    "Commerce emphasizes the study of business, finance, economics, and accounting. It includes subjects such as business studies, economics, accountancy, entrepreneurship, and mathematics. Students in the commerce stream learn about financial management, marketing, business laws, taxation, and other aspects of the business world. This branch prepares students for careers in areas like finance, banking, accounting, marketing, human resources, and entrepreneurship."

    humanities

    +

    "Humanities focuses on the study of human society, culture, languages, literature, arts, and social sciences. It includes subjects such as history, geography, political science, sociology, psychology, literature, languages, and philosophy. Students in the humanities stream explore human behavior, societal structures, historical events, cultural practices, and critical thinking skills. This branch prepares students for careers in fields like education, journalism, law, social work, humanities research, arts, and humanities-related professions."
